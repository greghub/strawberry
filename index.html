What is StrawberryCloud?

StrawberryCloud is a new attitude to having remote teams.
We are a team of professional developers and designers who will make your project a success.

Similar to cloud servers, we offer you scalable teams that change size according to the needs of your project.
Need a few more developers to complete the project more quickly? Wham, you've got them! Need some small fixes in the layout? You've got a designer. When you no longer need them, you don't pay for them.

Save your time and money, as we automatically bring in new members meeting the demands of your project.

How it works?

It works like Lego. Choose a main team e.g. {2 developers + 1 designer} or {5 developers + 2 designers} and scale the teams whenever you need.
